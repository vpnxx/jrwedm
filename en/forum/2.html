Usage of JiaRun products
 
 
JianRun Forum  >  FAQs
The main characteristics of JiaRun series products.

1). It has higher current working efficiency per unit time, more than 30 mm2/min•A under specific processing conditions. it is suitable for high current processing conditions. (The processing stability is achieved under the condition of an average machining current more than 5A);

2). In the processing of Cr12 and other die steel materials, the cutting surface is bright, silvery-white, homogeneous, and with hardly reversing stripes, under the condition of proper processing parameters, none reversing stripe cutting can be realized;

3). The washing and damping of the working liquid in the kerf ensure the cooling of the molybdenum wire and the machined surface, the probability of wire breakage is greatly decreased, and the special protective film greatly reduces the wear of molybdenum wire and prolongs its service life. At the same time, owing to the existence of damping of working liquid, absorbing the vibration of molybdenum wire, so the processing is very stable. this effect is particularly evident in the cutting of large thickness workpiece;

4). The eroded particles is not easy to dissolve with the working fluid, and can produce the natural precipitation effect, thus improving the service life of the working liquid;

5). The workpiece is easy to be picked up, and easy to be cleaned up with a cloth directly.
